FAQ

How are files handled?
Files never leave your device. OpenSig works from a cryptographic fingerprint of each file, not from uploads or shared copies.

What proof do stakeholders receive?
Every signing action or timestamp produces a shareable certificate that includes signer identity, intent, and a verifiable record reference. Anyone with the original file can confirm authenticity instantly.

Is my data secure?
Yes - completely. OpenSig is designed around privacy-first principles. Your files never leave your device, and your proofs reveal nothing about their contents. The only data published outside your device is a short, encrypted proof receipt ‚Äî not the file itself, nor any of your personal information.

Can I export or move my proofs?
Yes. OpenSig follows open formats and standards, so your records remain portable and verifiable even outside our platform.

What is the public record?
OpenSig anchors proofs to a public, tamper-proof blockchain ledger ‚Äî Polygon, a layer 2 blockchain built on Ethereum ‚Äî that anyone can verify independently. No personal data is stored on the ledger‚Äîonly cryptographic fingerprints of your files signed by your digital identity.
