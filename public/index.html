How OpenSig works

  4. Share with confidence. Anyone holding the file can confirm authenticity in seconds.

FAQ

Do we need to understand blockchain?
No. Think of OpenSig as a digital notary. We handle the cryptography and network costs behind the scenes.

How are files handled?
Files never leave your devices. OpenSig works from fingerprints, not file uploads.

What proof do stakeholders receive?
Each transaction produces a shareable receipt with signer identity, intent, and a verifiable timestamp.

Can we export our proofs?
Yes. Proofs follow open formats so you can validate or migrate them anytime.
