FAQ

How are files handled?
Files never leave your device. OpenSig works from a cryptographic fingerprint of each file, not from uploads or shared copies.

What proof do stakeholders receive?
Every signing action or timestamp produces a shareable certificate that includes signer identity, intent, and a verifiable record reference. Anyone with the original file can confirm authenticity instantly.

Can I export or move my proofs?
Yes. OpenSig follows open formats and standards, so your records remain portable and verifiable even outside our platform.

What is the public record?
OpenSig anchors proofs to a public, tamper-proof blockchain ledger ‚Äî Polygon ‚Äî that anyone can verify independently. No personal data is stored on the ledger‚Äîonly cryptographic fingerprints of your files signed by your digital identity.
