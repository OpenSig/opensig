Delete Your OpenSig Identity

You can delete your identity from within the OpenSig app. This will terminate your digital identity wallet and deregister you from all OpenSig services. All your data will be removed from our servers and you will no longer receive emails from us. Any remaining credits will be lost.

Click on your profile icon → Personal Details → Manage Your Identity.

If you have questions or concerns, or are having trouble deleting your identity, please contact us at support@opensig.net.
