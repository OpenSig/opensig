Deregister Your Identity

Deregistering your OpenSig identity will remove you from the email recovery service and communication preferences.

This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Your on-chain digital identity (DID) will remain intact, but:

You will no longer be able to recover your account
using your email address.

To deregister your OpenSig identity and have your associated data (email recovery and communication preferences) deleted, please send an email to the following address using the email address you signed up with:

deregister@opensig.net

If you have questions or concerns, please contact us at support@opensig.net.
